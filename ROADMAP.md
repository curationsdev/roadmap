## Status legend
- **Drafting / In-Progress** — actively being worked on
- **Planned** — scheduled for an upcoming quarter
- **Pondering** — early exploration / research
- **Maybe in the Future** — not realistic now but keeps the idea
- **Completed** — released
- **Delayed** — on hold
- **Pivot** — direction changed

We consider:
1. Community votes
2. Community impact and reach
3. Implementation effort and feasibility
4. Strategic alignment with CurationsLA goals
